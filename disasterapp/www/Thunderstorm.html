Thunderstorm

Thunderstorms are a type of storm that is known for its presence of lightning, which causes an effect in our atmosphere known as thunder. Thunderstorms are usually accompanied by strong winds, heavy rain, and possibly hail.

Warning Signs

Signs of thunderstorms include a rapid change in temperature and wind due to movements of warm, moist air. The formation of thick cumlonimbus clouds would aslo be apparent. Lightining strikes would become frequent across the sky in contrast to the darkness due to cloud cover.

Precautions for One's Safety:

One should concerned with flooding that may occur, you should remove valuables from low places such as basements. Power outages may occur, so having back up light such as candles and flashlights are desirable. One should unplug electronics to avoid damage to devices. Tornado formation is possible, along with heavy winds, hail, and flash flooding.
